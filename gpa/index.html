GPA Calculation Rules

At Ilia State University, GPA is calculated by dividing the sum of the products of Grade Point (GP) earned in each course and its credits by the total number of credits taken by the student.

GPA = Σ(Course GP × Course Credits) / Σ(Total Course Credits)

GP represents the converted score of a course grade on a 4-point scale.

Grade Score Range GP
A+ 96-100 4.00
A 91-95 3.92-4.00
B 81-90 3.50-3.92
C 71-80 2.67-3.50
D 61-70 1.83-2.67
E 51-60 1.00
F 0-50 0.00

Scores from 51-60 always equal 1.00 GP. Grades between 61 and 95 increase evenly by approximately 0.08. Scores from 96-100 always equal 4.00 GP.

Final GPA is rounded to the nearest tenth and is reflected in the diploma supplement.
